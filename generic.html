More About Me

I began my professional journey in Peru, earning my bachelor's degree and license in industrial engineering, where I applied my skills in logistics and project administration. After three years working across various sectors—transportation, retail, logistics, health, and services—I moved to the U.S. to enhance my administrative and management capabilities. During my master's studies, I developed a deep interest in data management, realizing the omnipresence and power of data here compared to my home country. This led me to pursue a master's in Business Analytics in New York, graduating with honors and a perfect 4.0 GPA. Since then, I've honed my proficiency in SQL, Python, and R, while continuously expanding my expertise in new technologies and AI.
